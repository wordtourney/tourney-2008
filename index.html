Word Tourney 2008

Word Tourney 2008 was the second word tourney following the massively popular 2007 tournament. This tournament had a different starting format from the previous year: Words were suggested by those invited to participate and then each participant selected 16 words they'd like to see compete. The bracket was then filled with the most popular words with the words with the most votes getting higher seeding. Words were then voted on based on their match up and the word with the most votes continued to the next round until one word remained.
